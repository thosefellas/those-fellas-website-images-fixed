About Ian Ascough & Those Fellas

Those Fellas' Founder and Director Ian Ascough has worked in football for over 30 years. With 15 years' worth of experience in the Chinese market, we are uniquely positioned to bridge the gap between Western football and the world's biggest market.

Based in England, we can attend club and player content days and create publishing plans that help disseminate content via our "Chinese Lens" approach, ensuring authentic cultural adaptation and maximum market impact.

We work with local, UK-based partners as well as our network of long-standing partners in China's football community, rights holders, key opinion leaders (KOLs) and media - both digital and linear.
